ESCAPE-FROM-BASH-STREET-SCHOOL

(Full Game)

PLATFORMS:
Browser and Mobile

ENGINE:
Construct 2

TOOLS USED:
Photoshop, Krita, Wacom/XP-Pen Tablet, Pyxel Edit

DURATION:
4 weeks

COMPLETION:
2019

TEAM SIZE:
5

ROLE:
Game Development/Design, Art, Implementation

Details

Escape from Bash Street School is a mobile and browser platforming game, where the player must find their way out of the secret basement of bash street school.

The game includes 9 unique playable characters and 15 levels, with 10 included on release and 5 added post-launch. The player is able to jump/double-jump by tapping their screen or mouse and can turn by bumping into geometry.
A variety of enemies and traps must be avoided while the player searches each level for a key to unlock the escape door. As the player progresses through the game they will unlock the cast of The Bash Street Kids such as Toots, Danny, Plug, Smiffy and more.
