Tower Marsh

PLATFORMS:
Windows PC

ENGINE:
Stronghold Legends Engine

TOOLS USED:
Stronghold Legends Scenario Editor

DURATION:
1 day

COMPLETION:
2017

TEAM SIZE:
Solo

ROLE:
Concept, Level Design, Testing

Details

Tower Marsh is a 4 player skirmish map suitable for multiplayer or ai skirmishes. Multiple heights of ground make capturing areas around the map beneficial to defend your home keep.
Below the heights of the 4 main keeps, is a marsh in the centre of the map. Moving through the marsh can be treacherous for an army, with multiple high vantage points surrounding the low lying marshes. On two sides, villages are caught in a crossfire, requiring players to wrestle control over the extra walls and resources.
The map was released in 2017 on the Steam workshop for Stronghold: Legends. Link: to the workshop page.
