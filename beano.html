FURTHER DETAILS

BEANO: FUN FORCE is a multiplayer mobile game for ages 7+ and is set in the Beano universe by Beano Studios

As the designer of the game I directed the overall vision alongside the team lead.

Primarily a comic, the Beano franchise is set around the character Dennis "the menace" and his friends in Beanotown.

BEANO: FUN FORCE was a student project made in collaboration/for our client Beano Studios of D.C Thompson.

PROCESS

Originally planned as a fast mini-game experience similar to the Warioware games, BEANO: FUN FORCE eventually became a slower more strategic game with more focus on learning mini-games and finding strategies to beat other players.

As the game was set in the BEANO universe, humour was an important aspect to translate into the video game format. This was achieved through both design choices within the game itself and aesthetic choices related to art.
