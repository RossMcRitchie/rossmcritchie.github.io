FURTHER DETAILS

Garden of Ouroboros is a 2D 'point-and-click' adventure game I worked on for the 2 day #JimJam2 game jam with as part of a team of 3 people during 2018.

As the team lead, I directed the overall vision and output required from the team across the 48 hour period. Alongside game design, I had the role of environmental artist, UI/UX Design and Scripter.

The game consists of 6 unique hand-drawn backdrops, a variety of puzzles to solve and interesting characters to meet and converse with.

PROCESS

During the first few hours of the jam plans were drawn up for the overall concept, including the long and short term goals the player would strive towards. A timeline was also created to keep the team on track during the jam.

Towards the deadline, some minor puzzles had to be removed and some puzzles reworked in order to remain consistant.

RELEASE

Released on itch.io on submission of the jam, the game was well recieved and ranked 11th of 146 entries.

The game can be found on itch.io : https://rosstheboss0.itch.io/garden-of-oroboros
