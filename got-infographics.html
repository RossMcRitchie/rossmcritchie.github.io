R/GAMEOFTHRONES INFOGRAPHICS

PLATFORMS:
r/gameofthrones

TOOLS USED:
Photoshop, Illustator, Reddit

DURATION:
3 weeks

COMPLETION:
2016

TEAM SIZE:
Solo

ROLE:
Using survey data to create a series of interesting infographics based on the tv series "Game of Thrones" by HBO

Details

During season 6 of HBO's "Game of Thrones", Reddit's r/gameofthrones held a post-episode survey, each week for 10 weeks. For 3 of these weeks I was asked to create infographics based on the survey results data on the Monday and have them released on the Friday.
The suveys were roughly taken by over 22,000 respondants each week. Each infographic was completed within 3 days, often having been planned and laid out on the Tuesday, approved on the Wednesday and submitted on the Thursday.
The infographics and the associated reddit posts can be found here.
