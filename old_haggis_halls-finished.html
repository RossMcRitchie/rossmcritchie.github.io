FURTHER DETAILS

Old Haggis Halls is a short isometric RPG made over the course of 3 months as a solo project. Made in GameMaker Studio 2.

As a solo project, all aspects of the game other than music and some sound effects were created by myself during the course of development.

PROCESS

The project began as an open world role-playing game similar to the original Zelda games, and inspired by the world design of 'Metroid-vania' style games, but soon evolved into a smaller, more focussed experience along similar lines.

The idea of the game was to create a vertical slice of an isometric 'metroid-vania' style rpg set in a Scottish styled theme.

RELEASE

The player must manage their stamina and health, while avoiding traps and combating or fleeing enemies. The player also has a selection of items or skills they can equip to suit their style of play, such as swords, bows or enchanted rings.

This game was released on itch.io in early 2019 after 2 months of full time development and 3 months of part-time development.
