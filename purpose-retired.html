FURTHER DETAILS

Purpose: Retired is a point-and-click adventure game made in 2017 about a man looking for purpose after retirement.

Made in just under two weeks, the game was developed in the AGS engine and was a solo project. My tasks involved everything from design, art, script-writing and testing.

The game contains multiple characters with dialog trees, various areas for the player to explore(as many as 15 unique backdrops) and a variety of different puzzles to solve.

PROCESS

The process in the games' creation involved a stage of concept generation, followed by pipeline testing, and finally development and testing.

Initially the concept simply involved the character of a retired police officer/parent and the idea that they would in some related manner combine their policing and parental instincts.

RELEASE

The game was initially released only in blog format, however in 2018 it was released on itch.io with some new promotional materials for viewing on a store page.

Since release the game has been updated with some minor changes after player feedback.

The game can be found on itch.io : https://rosstheboss0.itch.io/purpose-retired
